Privacy Policy

Tension Head  ·  Last updated: 03 March 2026

1. Introduction

Monodivision ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you play Tension Head (the "Game").

2. Information We Collect

The Game collects minimal information to provide core functionalities:

  • Gameplay Data We collect your chosen arcade name and high scores to populate the global leaderboard.
  • Device & Usage Data If you are using the free version of the Game, our third-party advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect your IP address and general device information solely to serve non-personalised advertisements. No advertising identifier (such as Apple IDFA) is used for ad targeting.
  • Purchase History In-app purchases are processed securely through the Apple App Store. We only receive a token confirming your purchase (e.g. unlocking the "Ad-Free" premium tier) and do not have access to your payment details.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Talo Used to host and manage our global leaderboards. Talo Privacy Policy →
  • Google AdMob Used to serve non-personalised advertisements in the free version of the Game. Google Privacy Policy →

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your arcade name and score on the public leaderboard.
  • To serve non-personalised advertisements (unless you have purchased the Ad-Free upgrade).

5. Data Deletion

If you wish to have your leaderboard data removed, please contact us at bob@monodivision.com with your exact arcade name and we will delete your record from our servers.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Game following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
